Circular Tech

Circular is not just technology, it is a fundamental mindset change. It's about seeing all the materials circulating around us as resources valuable for our economy, instead of waste, and about keeping their value for as long as possible.



We track your stuff

By tracking waste through small location awareness tags, while they travel seamlessly through a pervasive network of sensors -either existing or customized to needs of our clients-, we can locate, classify and measure our trash, enabling a waste management system that can scale from buildings and municipalities to cities or even entire countries. Such a system will help to reveal the final journey of every day objects, in a near real-time fashion, in order to return them back to the supply chain.
